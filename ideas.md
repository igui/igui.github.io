# Design Ideas for Ignacio Avas Portfolio — LIGHT THEME

## Selected Design: Minimalist Modern — Light & Airy

**Design Philosophy**: Clean, professional, and sophisticated. A light background with strategic accent colors, generous whitespace, and refined typography. The design emphasizes clarity and readability while maintaining a premium, innovative feel.

**Color Palette**:
- Background: Off-white (#FAFAF8)
- Text: Deep charcoal (#1A1A1A)
- Accent: Vibrant teal (#0EA5A5) and soft slate (#64748B)
- Cards: Pure white (#FFFFFF) with subtle shadows
- Borders: Light gray (#E5E7EB)

**Typography**: Space Grotesk (headings, bold and confident) + Outfit (body, clean and readable)

**Key Features**:
- Minimalist hero with large typography
- Glassmorphism cards with light backgrounds
- Smooth scroll animations and transitions
- Clean navigation with active indicators
- Professional gradient accents (teal-to-slate)
- Ample whitespace and breathing room
